# Hackathon QuipuSoft 2026 Context

## Theme

FAYE is being built for:

> Agentes de IA para incentivar los habitos de clasificacion y reciclado domestico.

The product should make domestic recycling simpler, more motivating, and easier to repeat.

## Problem

Households do not fail only because people lack information. The common breakdown is:

1. The user is unsure what kind of waste they have.
2. The user does not know the correct preparation step.
3. The action is not reinforced as a habit.
4. The positive impact is not visible.

FAYE should solve the full loop: discover, understand, act, record, motivate, repeat.

## Relevant Challenge Areas

- Automatic waste identification with AI.
- Educational conversational assistance, expressed as an agent, not a generic chatbot.
- Gamification through progress, streaks, points, and small rewards.
- Personalization from user behavior.
- Individual environmental impact measurement.
- Simple and inclusive user experience.
- Scalable adoption for homes, schools, communities, or municipalities.

## Evaluation Lens

The solution must score across:

- Innovation and use of AI.
- Environmental and social impact.
- Technical and economic feasibility.
- Functionality.
- Usability and accessibility.
- Scalability and long-term sustainability.

## Demo Implication

The jury should see Eve working in the product flow:

1. A residue enters through camera, upload, or a seeded demo item.
2. Eve identifies it with confidence and context.
3. Eve explains the category and preparation step.
4. The user confirms the action.
5. FAYE records progress and makes impact visible.

This is why FAYE is not a chatbot-first app. Eve is the intelligence layer that turns uncertainty into a concrete habit action.
